This is a WIP.

Only the Start and Help button work. Press Back to go back.

Inside the main sim, pan with the RMB, and hold the wheel OR hold ` to open a radial menu.

Click with the food tool (apple icon) to place food, drag a lizard by the head with the drag tool (lizard hand icon), and click on a lizard with the inspect tool (maginifying glass) to check a lizard's information.

Press ESC to pause.

You can also open the console with / and enter one of the following commands:

visionCone
hearingRange
senseArea
hitboxGrid
showId
drawPath
teleport OR tp
